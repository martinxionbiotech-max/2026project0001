FAQ: Next Blood Moon Checker

What is a blood moon?

A blood moon is the reddish Moon seen during a total lunar eclipse when Earth’s atmosphere filters sunlight and bends red light onto the Moon.

How accurate is this Next Blood Moon Checker?

The countdown and conversion are time-accurate for the listed UTC event timestamps. Visibility is shown by country from the included dataset.

Can I check next blood moon time in local timezone?

Yes. The tool shows both local time and UTC for each upcoming event, so you can plan observation in your own timezone.

Why display days left, not just date?

Most users want a quick answer to "how long until the next blood moon". The days-left countdown is more intuitive than reading a timestamp alone.

Can I use this on phone while traveling?

Yes. Tap "Use My Location" after you arrive at your destination to refresh country selection and review likely visibility.
